About Us

Our Vision

The KITS ACM student chapter program will become the primary source for undergraduate computer science career development and the premier network of computing chapters at institutions of higher education across the globe.

Our Mission

The KITS ACM student chapter program promotes the study, application, and advancement of computer science through career.
